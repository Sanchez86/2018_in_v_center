Essential CFD Strategies in Forex Trading

We recommend you get acquainted with the following list of trading strategies that have been the most effective on the market for decades. If your intention is to make smart moves and succeed against the market, the strategies below may become your strongest assistant and the foundation in developing further, much more sophisticated strategies.

Bollinger Bands

This particular approach is among the leaders of using in CFD online trading. Over the years this tool has proven its value and efficiency. The Bollinger Bands strategy displays the conditions for a good entrance with two bands - blue and red. Traders consider it as a signal to open trades when the asset`s price touches the red band (buy) or blue band (sell). Novice traders will definitely find this strategy useful and convenient.

RSI (Relative Strength Indicator)

Also a powerful tool especially useful for beginners, RSI determines whether markets are bullish or bearish. The usage of this indicator is pretty simple. When the indicator`s line rises higher than 30 point, this is a good opportunity to buy the asset. However, when the RSI line goes below 70 point, it is recommended to start selling. A simple and effective tool, a must in every trader`s arsenal.

MACD Moving Average Convergence Divergence

The MACD is among the most used technical indicators. It is displayed in the form of a chart with two moving averages of prices. This trend-following momentum indicator shows the relationship between the two averages. There is also a histogram with more functions which may be very suitable for advanced definitely find it perfectly suited for their trades.

Fibonacci Target Zones

Fibonacci tool is frequently used by traders to determine areas that will bring in profits. It shows the highest points of a trend on a taken time-frame. Using the Fibonacci lines traders can predict spaces of support and resistance for a certain asset, it`s potential behavior in the nearest future and this way, be ready to start buying or selling. One of the most powerful tools that allows traders to find a well-calculated solution in the most tangled situations.

ParSAR

The parabolic SAR is used by lots of traders to determine the direction of an asset's momentum and the point in time when this momentum has a higher-than-normal probability of switching directions.
